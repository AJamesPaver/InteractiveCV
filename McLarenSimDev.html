Simulation Development

Data empowers decision making.

Data is everything in Formula 1.

No other sport (and few other industries!) has such a reliance on data as we do.

Every piece of science and mathematics is used to make a decision which may make us more competitive.

If we can provide the best simulations and analytics, and deliver this data to the engineers who need it as fast as possible, then we have the advantage over our competitors!

This is the role of Simulation Development.

As part of the Simulation Development team, I use a variety of tools and languages to fulfill this role.

We are experts in MATLAB and C++, and make extensive use of Python and MySQL also.

Machine learning is also heavily used in everything we do - we develop our own bespoke machine learning algorithms.

I lead the projects for our inference tools and our live data processing - delivering simulation data to our engineers in real-time as the car is on track.

Live, On-Track Data

I look after the on-track simulation data at McLaren, what we call "Live Data".

The McLaren Formula 1 car has hundreds of sensors; including temperatures, pressures, forces, displacements and many more. This data is streamed back to the garage when the car is on track via the telemetry system.

But that is where the fun begins. There is so much more that we can do with this data. We run simulations on the telemetry data to create greater insights for our engineers - all in real time!
