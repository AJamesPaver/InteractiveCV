My Projects

Take a look below at some examples of the kind of projects I have been involved in.

1D Electric Vehicle Simulation

My team and I develop the primary 1D simulation tool at Fortescue Zero, called DCS or "Drive Cycle Simulation".

Take a look at this example simulation of a battery electric haul truck moving payload down a hill.

The example shows the basic physics of a 1D vehicle simulation and how regen is used when travelling downhill to regenerate energy and store it in the battery.

Find out more...

Longitudinal Train Dynamics Simulation

As part of a new battery-electric heavy haul locomotive project, we identified the requirement to simulate and predict the in-train forces for the train.

This simulation, together with 3D animation and data visualisation, shows a battery-electric haul train travelling uphill and downhill with unloaded and loaded ore cars.

It predicts the in-train forces together with the battery state-of-charge and energy usage for the route, coupling the electrical, mechanical and operational domains.

Find out more...

Live, On-Track Data

I looked after the on-track simulation data at McLaren, what was called "Live Data".

A Formula 1 car has hundreds of sensors; including temperatures, pressures, forces, displacements and many more. This data is streamed back to the garage when the car is on track via the telemetry system.

But that is where the fun begins. There is so much more that we can do with this data. We run simulations on the telemetry data to create greater insights for our engineers - all in real time!
